Hyderabad

Explore the rich cultural heritage of Hyderabad, a city known for its historical landmarks and vibrant traditions. This heritage site, with its majestic architecture and captivating history, reflects the essence of the city.

Charminar

The iconic Charminar, built in 1591 by Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah, stands proudly at the heart of the city. Its four grand arches offer breathtaking views of the bustling bazaars surrounding it.

Golconda Fort

Golkonda Fort, a marvel of military architecture, whispers tales of the Qutb Shahi dynasty. Explore the ruins and feel the echoes of history resonating through the fort's stone walls.

It is Telangana's largest and most-populous city and is the major urban centre for all of south-central interior India. From 1956 to 2014 Hyderabad was the capital of Andhra Pradesh state, but, with the creation of Telangana from Andhra Pradesh in 2014, it was redesignated as the capital of both states.
