Ramoji Film City

Step into the enchanting world of cinema at Ramoji Film City, the world's largest film studio complex. Nestled in the heart of Hyderabad, Ramoji Film City offers a mesmerizing blend of entertainment, recreation, and the magic of movie-making.

As you enter through the grand gates, you'll be greeted by the spectacular landscapes, themed sets, and the vibrant energy that defines the film city. Explore the diverse film sets, each telling a unique story and creating a magical atmosphere.

From intricately designed streets to magnificent palaces, Ramoji Film City transports you to different eras and locations. Capture the behind-the-scenes moments and witness the art of filmmaking unfold in this cinematic wonderland.

Attractions at Ramoji Film City

Discover the various attractions that make Ramoji Film City a unique destination:

  • Movie Magic: Witness live film shootings and explore the fascinating world of filmmaking.
  • Themed Gardens: Stroll through beautifully landscaped gardens with unique themes.
  • Spectacular Shows: Enjoy live performances, cultural shows, and entertaining events.
  • Adventure Park: Experience thrill rides and adventure activities for an adrenaline rush.

Visitor Information

Plan your visit to Ramoji Film City with the following information:

  • Location: Anajpur, Hayathnagar Mandal, Hyderabad, Telangana, India.
  • Timings: Open daily from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
  • Entry Fee: Check the official website for the latest ticket prices and packages.
  • Facilities: Restaurants, souvenir shops, and comfortable accommodations are available within the film city.
